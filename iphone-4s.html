iPhone 4S (2011)

The iPhone 4S was the first iPhone to have Siri. On October 4, 2011, Apple unveiled the iPhone 4S. The day after the presentation, Apple founder Steve Jobs died. The S in the name stood for "Siri" as it was an exclusive feature for this iPhone when it came out.

  • Apple A5 SoC
  • 512 MB RAM
  • 3.5" Retina LCD screen
  • Weight: 140 g
