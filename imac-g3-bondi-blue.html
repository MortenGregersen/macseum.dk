iMac G3 Bondi Blue (1998)

The first iMac. It came in several revisions. The first revision came in only one color - Bondi Blue.

  • PowerPC G3 233 MHz processor
  • 32 MB RAM
  • 4 GB hard drive
  • 24x CD-rom-drev
  • 15" CRT screen
  • Weight: 17,2 kg
