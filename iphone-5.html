iPhone 5 (2012)

The big news about the iPhone 5 was that the screen had gotten a little higher. It had the same width as the previous iPhones, but had grown from 3.5" to 4". Despite the larger screen, this iPhone weighed less than all the previous ones. The iPhone 5 was the first device to use Apple's Lightning connectors.

  • Apple A6 SoC
  • 1.3 GHz processor
  • 1 GB RAM
  • 4" Retina LCD screen
  • Weight: 112 g
