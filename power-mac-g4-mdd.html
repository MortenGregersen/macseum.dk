Power Mac G4 Mirrored Drive Doors (2002)

The year after the Power Mac G4 Quicksilver came out, another new version of the Power Mac came out with a new design and better processors. This one weighed over 5 kg more than its predecessors.

  • PowerPC G4 1 GHz processor
  • 256 MB RAM
  • 60 GB hard drive
  • CD/DVD burner
  • Weight: 19,1 kg
