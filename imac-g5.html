iMac G5 (2004)

Only 2 years after the iMac G4 was launched, there was a refresh. Back then, the computer was built into the screen. This design is largely the same as we have now in the latest iMacs.

  • PowerPC G5 2.1 GHz processor
  • 512 MB RAM
  • 250 GB hard drive
  • SuperDrive
  • 20" LCD screen
  • Weight: 10 kg
