iMac G3 Blueberry (1999)

Half a year after the first iMac G3 Bondi Blue was launched, there were a few revisions of the iMac in 5 fresh "fruit- colors ". The first revision had a "tray-loading" optical drive - the subsequent one had a "slot-loading" CD-ROM drive.

  • PowerPC G3 400 MHz processor
  • 64 MB RAM
  • 6 GB hard drive
  • 4x DVD-rom drive
  • 15" CRT screen
  • Weight: 15.7 kg
