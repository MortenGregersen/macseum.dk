MacBook Air 11" (2011)

The smallest Mac ever made is this MacBook Air 11". With its weight of just about 1 kg, it is easy to carry under the arm. However, it has probably nowadays lost a lot of market share to the iPad, but compared to its time, it was a wild little machine.

  • Intel Core i5 1.6 GHz processor
  • 4 GB RAM
  • 128 GB hard drive
  • 11" LCD screen
  • Weight: 1 kg
