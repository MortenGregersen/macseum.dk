Aluminum PowerBook G4 (2003)

In 2001, Apple introduced their 15" PowerBook G4 made of titanium. 2 years later it came in aluminum and at the same time also in 12" and 17". It was a very different design than the PowerBook G3s made of plastic.

  • PowerPC G4 1 GHz processor
  • 512 MB RAM
  • 60 GB hard drive
  • DVD-RW SuperDrive
  • 17 "TFT LCD screen
  • Weight: 3.1 kg
