PowerBook G3 (1998)

At the end of the 1990s, Apple introduced a series of PowerBooks based on the PowerPC G3. This one is the second generation also called the "Wallstreet I".

  • PowerPC G3 250 MHz processor
  • 32 MB RAM
  • 2 GB hard drive
  • CD/DVD drive
  • 14.1 "TFT screen
  • Weight: 3.5 kg
