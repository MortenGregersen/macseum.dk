iMac G4 (2002)

The first redesign of the popular iMac. It was the first iMac with a flat screen.

  • PowerPC G4 800 MHz processor
  • 256 MB RAM
  • 60 GB hard drive
  • DVD and CD-ROM drive / burner
  • 15" TFT LCD screen
  • Weight: 9.7 kg
