Unibody iMac (2009)

In 2009, Apple introduced an iMac with a 16:9 sceen, made out of one piece of aluminium. It was the last iMac to include an internal SuperDrive.

  • Intel Core 2 Duo 3.06 GHz processor
  • 4 GB RAM
  • 500 GB hard drive
  • 8x double-layer SuperDrive
  • 21.5" LCD screen
  • Weight: 9.3 kg
