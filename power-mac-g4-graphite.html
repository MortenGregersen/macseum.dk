Power Mac G4 Graphite (1999)

After the Power Mac G3 Blue & White came a new model with the PowerPC G4. This one was very similar to the previous model, but in more muted colors.

  • PowerPC G4 400 MHz processor
  • 128 MB RAM
  • 20 GB hard drive
  • CD/DVD drive
  • Weight: 13.6 kg
