iPod Touch 4. generation (2010)

A few months after the introduction of the iPhone 4, Apple introduced a new iPod Touch. It had almost the same specs as the iPhone 4 and the same retina display.

  • Apple A4 SoC
  • 256 MB RAM
  • 8 GB hard drive
  • 3.5" Retina LCD screen
  • Weight: 101 g
