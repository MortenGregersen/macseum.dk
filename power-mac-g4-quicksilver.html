Power Mac G4 Quicksilver (2001)

In 2001 came a revised version of the Power Mac G4 Graphite with new design and better processors.

  • PowerPC G4 800 MHz processor
  • 256 MB RAM
  • 40 GB hard drive
  • CD/DVD burner
  • Weight: 13.6 kg
