Ever since the first Macintosh in 1984, mice have been used to control Macs. With the iMacG3 came the "much-loved" Apple USB Mouse also known as "Hockey Puck". It came in the same colors as the iMac. It was later replaced by the Apple Pro Mouse in white and black, the Apple Mighty Mouse and the Apple Magic Mouse.
