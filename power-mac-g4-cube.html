Power Mac G4 Cube (2000)

At the turn of the millennium, Apple introduced a 8x8x10 inch (20x20x25 cm) Cube. It was presented as a small machine but with the same power as a large Power Mac G4. The cube is on display at the Museum of Modern Art in New York.

  • PowerPC G4 450 MHz processor
  • 128 MB RAM
  • 20 GB hard drive
  • CD burner / DVD drive
  • Weight: 6.4 kg
