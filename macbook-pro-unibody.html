MacBook Pro Unibody (2008)

The first Mac I bought was the Unibody MacBook Pro. What set it apart from its predecessor and other laptops at the time was that the case was made of one piece of aluminum.

  • Intel Core 2 Duo 2.53 GHz processor

  • 250 GB hard drive
  • DVD-RW SuperDrive
