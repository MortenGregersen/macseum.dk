iMac G4 for the kids (2002)

Kids have their own iMac G4 to play the old Pixeline games on. I had otherwise offered them a newer iMac, but they liked this model best.

  • PowerPC G4 800 MHz processor
  • 256 MB RAM
  • 60 GB hard drive
  • DVD and CD-ROM drive / burner
  • 15" TFT LCD screen
  • Weight: 9.7 kg
