iPod Touch 2nd generation (2008)

A few months after the introduction of the iPhone 3G, Apple introduced a new iPod Touch. It had almost the same specs as the iPhone 3G just without the phone part.

  • Samsung 412 MHz processor
  • 128 MB RAM
  • 32 GB hard drive
  • 3.5" LCD screen
  • Weight: 115 g
