iMac G3 Tangerine (1999)

Half a year after the first iMac G3 Bondi Blue was launched, there were a few revisions of the iMac in 5 fresh "fruit colors". The first revision had a "tray-loading" CD-ROM drive - the subsequent one had a "slot-loading" CD-ROM drive.

  • PowerPC G3 266 MHz processor
  • 32 MB RAM
  • 6 GB hard drive
  • 24x CD-rom-drev
  • 15" CRT screen
  • Weight: 17,2 kg
