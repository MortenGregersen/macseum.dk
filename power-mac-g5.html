Power Mac G5 (2002)

In 2003, Apple stopped updating the Power Mac G4 line and instead launched a new redesigned machine with the powerful PowerPC G5 processor.

  • PowerPC G5 1.8 GHz processor
  • 512 MB RAM
  • 160 GB hard drive
  • SuperDrive
  • Weight: 20.1 kg
