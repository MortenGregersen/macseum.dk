iPhone 7 (2016)

When Apple introduced the iPhone 7, they introduced a phone that looked like the previous model (iPhone 6S) in new colors, but there was more in it. It no longer had a mechanical home button, it was much more water and dust repellent, but "worst" of all, it had no 3.5 "jack connector for headphones. It gave a lot of criticism, but it seems to be forgotten now.

  • Apple A10 Fusion SoC
  • 2.34 GHz processor
  • 2 GB RAM
  • 4.7" Retina LCD screen
  • Weight: 138 g
