Power Mac G3 Blue & White (1999)

In conjunction with the beautiful new iMacs, Apple also introduced a new professional tower. The previous models had the classic beige cabinets, but this model had some of the same new design elements such as transparency and colors. Because of the colors, it also got the nickname "Blue and White".

  • PowerPC G3 350 MHz processor
  • 64 MB RAM
  • 20 GB hard drive
  • CD/DVD drive
  • Weight: 13 kg
