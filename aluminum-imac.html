Aluminum iMac (2007)

In 2006-2007, Apple switched from using PowerPC processors to using Intel processors in their Macs. The first iMac with Intel processor looked like the iMac G5 at one point, but the following year a new iMac came out - this time in aluminum instead of plastic like all its older siblings.

  • Intel Core 2 Duo G5 2.1 GHz processor
  • 1 GB RAM
  • 250 GB hard drive
  • 8x double-layer SuperDrive
  • 20" LCD screen
  • Weight: 9.1 kg
